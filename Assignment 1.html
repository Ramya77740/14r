Uppalapati Venkata Suryanarayana Prabhas Raju

([pɾabʱaːs] born 23 October 1979), known mononymously as Prabhas, is an Indian actor who works predominantly in Telugu cinema.[4] Referred to in the media as the "Rebel Star", he is one of the highest-paid actors in Indian cinema.[5] Prabhas has been featuring in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list since 2015[6][7][8] and has received seven Filmfare Awards nominations, a Nandi Award, and a SIIMA Award.He has received seven Filmfare Awards South nominations and is a recipient of the Nandi Award and the SIIMA Award. Prabhas is the second Indian actor to receive the “Russian Audience Heart Award” in 2020, after veteran actor Raj Kapoor who won it 30 years ago.

Success
